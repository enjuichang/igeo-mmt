# Population pyramids: 2026

200 PopulationPyramid.net country/area images, classified by age-profile shape and paired with the current World Bank income group returned by its API at generation time.

## Classification method

- **High-growth pyramid:** Broad base: the average 0–14 cohort is at least 15% wider than the average 25–49 cohort.
- **Bullet / column:** Near-column or transitional: the base-to-core cohort-width ratio is between 0.80 and 1.15.
- **Low-growth / constrictive:** Narrow base: the average 0–14 cohort is at least 20% narrower than the average 25–49 cohort.

The shape is calculated from the population data. Income is a separate descriptive field; it does not determine the shape classification.
